## Keyboard & Mouse Controls

You can navigate through the moves quickly using your mouse wheel or keyboard. 

### Mouse Scroll
* `Scroll Up`: Navigates forwards to the next move.
* `Scroll Down`: Navigates backwards to the previous move.

### Arrow Keys
* `Arrow Right`: Navigates forwards to the next move.
* `Arrow Left`: Navigates backwards to the previous move.
* `Arrow Down`: Resets the board to the starting position.
